# Source Context

This repo was seeded from public-facing and local source material about GAJRA Earth, Aura of Intelligence, C-Hours, public ledgers, event engines, travel pathways, legal memory and civic participation.

The public site should stay clear about status:

- GAJRA Earth is a founder-originated public prototype, not an already operating global organisation.
- The earlier Live Aid 2025 idea is historical concept material, not an event that was organised.
- The GAJRA token and DAO are future concepts only. Any real public token, sale, staking, rewards or investment wording needs formal legal review.
- C-Hour is intentionally presented as a non-speculative contribution record, not as a traded financial asset.
- Public pages can invite participation, but they should not imply official partnerships, government endorsement, clinical approval or active fundraising unless those exist.

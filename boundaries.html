Boundaries

Truth is infrastructure.

Boundaries are not a brake on imagination. They are how a visionary public project earns enough trust to survive contact with real people.

Status truth

Say what is real.

This site should be bold about the possibility and plain about the present status.

Founder-originated

GAJRA Earth is a founder-originated public prototype and activation pathway.

Not launched

The earlier ICO, DAO and Live Aid 2025 ideas were not launched or actively organised in the real world.

Research incoming

The site is ready to absorb deeper research, but current pages should not pretend that research is already integrated.

Risk rails

Where public wording must stay careful.

These areas can be explored, but public pages need clear wording and evidence.

Crypto and tokens

No investment advice, token sale, staking, yield, price, exchange, bonus or governance claim without formal legal review.

Health and clinical work

No diagnosis, treatment, therapeutic promise or clinical approval claim without qualified review and evidence.

Government and partners

No endorsement, partnership or official pathway claim unless it is documented and current.

Cultural authority

Local and First Nations protocols belong to the people and bodies with authority. This site can ask better questions, not replace consent.

Public wallets

Wallet transparency does not replace accounting, tax, charity law, contracts or event permitting.

Private notes

Personal data, sensitive health stories, unapproved images and private planning detail do not belong on public pages.

Public/private split

Useful in public, deeper in private.

The public site should invite contribution and show source trails. The private workbench can hold messier drafts, identity detail, sensitive records and unfinished strategy.

Public pages can show

Actions, templates, source titles, public URLs, contribution cards, event summaries, C-Hour totals, wallet notes and clear caveats.

Private notes should hold

Personal contact details, raw anxieties, private health material, unapproved partnership targets, draft token mechanics and unresolved legal analysis.

Boundaries

Clear rails let big ideas move without losing public trust.

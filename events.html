Events

A decentralised event engine.

The event layer begins with local hosts and public-safe records, then grows into festivals, summits, travel routes and shared value votes when the trust is real enough.

Operating loop

Invite, gather, record, care.

Every event can be small, but the pattern should be repeatable.

1. Invitation

Say who the gathering is for, what question it asks and what is definitely not being promised.

2. Local table

Collect definitions, needs, offers, stories and value words in the language of the place.

3. Public record

Publish a simple after-action note, C-Hours, public wallet movement and next actions.

4. Aftercare

Check consent, safety, cultural protocols, privacy and whether the action actually helped.

Event scales

From shed table to world stage.

The earlier Live Aid 2025 idea becomes concept history and future inspiration, not a claim that it happened.

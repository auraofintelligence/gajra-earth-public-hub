Actions

Do something real before explaining the universe.

No one needs the full architecture to enter. Pick a move, make it legible, leave a trace.

Action ladder

From ten minutes to a local circle.

Small enough to start. Structured enough to matter later.

10 minutes

Name the gap

What is broken, missing or absurd where you are?

1 hour

Run one useful errand

A cleanup, call, intro, document, table, repair, ride, song, map or note.

1 day

Host a table

Invite a few people around a real question. Capture values, needs, offers and next moves.

1 month

Start a circle

Repeat the table. Publish a short trace. Connect to another place or project.

Roles

Choose your lane.

Different skills, different risk, same commons.

Contributor

Shares a definition, public trace, useful hour, artwork, story, local need or live link.

Host

Runs a small table, gathering, workshop, festival corner or online circle with clear consent.

Steward

Keeps claims clean, consent explicit, safety practical and public wording honest.

Researcher

Turns long reports into readable public summaries with source trails and caveats.

Builder

Improves pages, forms, ledgers, event templates, data exports and accessibility.

Sponsor

Funds practical public goods without buying the commons memory.
